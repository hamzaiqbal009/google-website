Sohan Halwah Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup rosewater
  • 1 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 cup pistachios, chopped

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix the flour and vegetable oil together in a bowl until well combined.
  3. Add the cardamom powder and chopped pistachios to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Place the mixture in a baking dish and bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. While the sohan halwah is baking, prepare the syrup by combining the sugar, water, and rosewater in a saucepan over medium heat.
  6. Bring the syrup to a boil, stirring constantly until the sugar has dissolved.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and let the syrup simmer for 10 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  8. When the sohan halwah is done, remove it from the oven and pour the hot syrup over it.
  9. Let the sohan halwah cool and absorb the syrup before serving.
